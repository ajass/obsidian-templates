## Linking & Tagging Guide

### Tags to Consider Adding:
- `#quote/[topic]` - Topic categorization: `#quote/leadership`, `#quote/grief`
- `#quote/author/[name]` - Group quotes by author
- `#quote/fiction` / `#quote/non-fiction` - Source type
- `#inspiration` - Quotes that motivate action

### Linking Tips:
- **Concept links**: Link the quote to the ideas it represents: `[[personal-growth]]`, `[[wisdom]]`
- **Source connections**: Link to other notes about the same book/person/film
- **Thematic clusters**: Link to other quotes on similar themes
- **Application notes**: Link to where you might use this quote: `[[future-essay-draft]]`

### Move Toward Evergreen:
1. Add at least 3 concept links
2. Note why this quote matters to you specifically
3. Link to related quotes or create stubs for future quotes
4. Consider the inverse: what does the opposite perspective look like?
